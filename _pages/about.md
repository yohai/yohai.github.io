We are a research group at the [Racah Institue of physics](https://phys.huji.ac.il) and the [School of Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.cs.huji.ac.il/) at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem.
We study physics of complex systems using tools from statistical physics, non linear mechanics, machine learning and computational physics.
